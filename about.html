About WINNING STAR

Company History & Global Exhibitions

Development History

1999

Setting Sail in Dubai

Established United Arab Emirates Winning Star Trading Co., Ltd. in Dubai, the "Capital of Trade", marking our official global debut.

2000

Yiwu Representative Office

Established the Yiwu Representative Office of Winning Star to act as a key market procurement and trade agency.

2011

Empowering Overseas Markets

Established overseas branch offices, extending into the African market to provide diverse quality products and bring light & hope to local families.

2013

Yiwu Import & Export Headquarters

Yiwu Yingxing Import & Export Co., Ltd. was established and designated as our main Yiwu Headquarters to centralize global export operations.

2016

Supply Chain Optimization

Officially changed our corporate name to Yiwu Yingxing Supply Chain Management Co., Ltd. to foster industrial clusters and global win-win partnerships.

2018

Launch of Global Small Appliances

Launched our premium global brand small home appliances project, driving deep integration of research, manufacturing, and international trade.

2020

NEWVEW Brand & Digital Leap

Established the "NEWVEW" brand, officially expanding our reach into the smart digital electronics and electronic accessories markets.

2021

Yiwu World Trade Center

Moved into the landmark Yiwu World Overseas Chinese Business Tower, building a brand new channel to champion the dual-circulation global development pattern.

2023+

Building the "Star Home" Eco-chain

Looking ahead to integrate our intelligent appliances, electronics, and home items to build a comprehensive "Star Home" ecological chain for global consumers.
